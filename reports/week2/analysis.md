## Learning Points
- concept document and feature's list must be prepared first
- 5 programmers is too much for the development
- core game loop must be implemented first
- digging using LMB is boring for users
- crafting system should be simple
  
## Validated assumptions
- we assumed that the weight of resources and overloading mechanic would engage players - rejected during the customer meeting
- we assumed that the users will dig using LMB - rejected during the customer meeting
- we assumed that the crafting mechanic will employ only amount of resources needed for some tool without drawings of the recipie (like in Minecraft) - confirmed during the customer meeting
- we assumed that the users need manual saving - confirmed after prototype development
- we assumed that the users need "drop out of the inventory" mechanic - rejected after testing in MVP v0
  
## Needs clarification
- inventory layout
- intresting mechanic for digging
- metrics for leaderboard
- upgrade tree
- warehouse and workshop room presence
